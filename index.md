## About me

Welcome to my personal website! I am a passionate _sports analyst_ with a strong focus on transforming data into meaningful insights that support performance, strategy, and decision‑making in sport.

I have experience developing analytical workflows that range from exploratory analysis to predictive and explanatory models, using machine‑learning approaches to **identify patterns, forecast outcomes, and support evidence‑based conclusions**. A key aspect of my work is translating these models into **clear, interpretable insights** that can be used by practitioners, rather than treating analytics as a purely technical exercise.

Through this site, I share projects, tools, and resources that reflect this approach to sports analytics, where rigorous modelling techniques, practical applications, and visual storytelling come together to highlight trends, patterns, and competitive advantages. Indeed, I am particularly interested in _how data can bridge the gap between theory and practice, supporting coaches, athletes, and organizations in understanding performance more deeply._

This platform serves both as a portfolio of my work and a space to explore emerging ideas in sports analytics, combining technical rigour with a clear, practical perspective.
